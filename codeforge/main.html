Welcome To CODE FORGE

(The technical club of NIAMT )

(Managed by ECE Department)

CodeForge is a dynamic community that fosters collaboration, innovation, and skill development among technology enthusiasts. It serves as a platform where individuals with diverse technical backgrounds come together to work on coding projects, knowledge, and solve real-world problems. Under CodeForge, multiple technical clubs focus on various domains such as software development, web design, artificial intelligence, and cybersecurity. These clubs encourage hands-on learning, coding competitions, and collaborative projects, providing a nurturing environment for both beginners and experienced tech enthusiasts. CodeForge promotes growth, and technical excellence, making it an ideal hub for aspiring developers.

Groups under CODEFORGE focusing on technologies and innovation:

COMMUNITY OF SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT

COMMUNITY OF ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE AND DATA SCIENCE

COMMUNITY OF CYBERSECURITY & BLOCKCHAIN

COMMUNITY OF MEDIA CELL

COMMUNITY OF ELECTRONICS AND AUTOMATION
